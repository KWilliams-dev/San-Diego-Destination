Brief Overview



One of my favorite things to do in San Diego, CA is surf. The waves are perfect for beginners. Although, it's still very difficult to get the hang of it. While your waiting for a wave, you can enjoy the scenery around you. There's palm trees on the board walk, hills/cliffs in the distance, and perfect weather.



Food

The food in San Diego is incredible. I didn't have one bad tasting meal last time I was there. From tauqitos, to bowls of ramen, you can pretty much find any type of food there that you like. My personal favorite was the taquitos that I got from this little hole in the wall place.



Skating

Not everyone enjoys this but another really fun thing to do is skate the boardwalk. It's also a lot of fun because you won't be the only one. Many people bring out there skates, skateboards, or bikes to enjoy the scenery at a faster pace. You just have to watch out for other people since it's usually a little busy.
