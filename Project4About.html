About San Diego



History

San Diego is the first city on the west coast that is entered upon crossing the mexico border into the united states. California did not become a part of the U.S. until 1850. Joshua H. Bean was elected the first mayor of the city. The area known as Old Town San Diego State Historic Park was once the original town of San Diego.

Weather

The weather is beautiful in San Diego. It stays at around 75 degrees during the summer. The only down side is that the ocean water is very cold so it's very important to wear a wet suit.
